## 📝 Gap Types Explained

### 1. Liquidity Gap
**What:** Liquid assets vs liabilities  
**Risk:** Can we meet short-term obligations?  
**Action:** Maintain adequate liquid assets

### 2. Interest Rate Gap
**What:** Rate-sensitive assets vs liabilities  
**Risk:** How sensitive are we to rate changes?  
**Action:** Balance rate sensitivity or hedge

### 3. Maturity Gap
**What:** Asset maturities vs liability maturities  
**Risk:** Timing mismatch of cash flows  
**Action:** Match maturities strategically

### 4. Duration Gap
**What:** Duration-weighted gap  
**Risk:** Long-term interest rate risk  
**Action:** Duration matching or hedging

5. EXPORT → Download for regulatory submission

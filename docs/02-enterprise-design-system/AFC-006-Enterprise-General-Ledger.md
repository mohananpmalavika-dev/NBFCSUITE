# AFC-006 — Enterprise General Ledger (EGL)

## Overview

The General Ledger is the financial source of truth for ARTH.OS. It stores every financial impact generated by the Journal Engine and becomes the authoritative repository for balances, reporting, compliance, and audit.

Unlike traditional ERPs, ARTH.OS should treat the ledger as an immutable, multi-dimensional, multi-book, event-driven financial record rather than a simple balance table.

---

## Vision

The General Ledger must support:

- Multi-book accounting
- Real-time ledger posting
- Event-driven financial processing
- Immutable ledger history
- Multi-dimensional ledger analysis
- Multi-currency accounting
- AI-powered financial intelligence

The core principle is simple: journals represent business intent, while the ledger records the permanent financial truth.

## Major Architectural Recommendation

ARTH.OS should introduce a Financial Data Lake and Ledger Warehouse alongside the operational General Ledger.

### Recommended Architecture

```text
Accounting Event
↓
Journal Engine
↓
Operational General Ledger
↓
Financial Data Warehouse
├── AI Analytics
├── Regulatory Reporting
├── MIS
└── Executive Dashboards
```

This separation provides:

- Fast operational posting without slowing reporting
- Complex analytics without impacting transaction performance
- Historical snapshots for audit and trend analysis
- Easier RBI, statutory, GST, and management reporting
- AI models trained on clean financial history rather than live transactional tables

# Enterprise Organization Management (EOM)

## Business Purpose

EOM establishes the enterprise operating structure for the platform, including legal entities, business units, branches, departments, grades, positions, and approval flows.

## Scope

- Organization hierarchy
- Branch and department management
- Position and designation control
- Cost and profit center mapping
- Approval matrix and org chart

## Core Entities

- Legal entity
- Business unit
- Zone
- Region
- Area
- Cluster
- Branch
- Department
- Grade
- Position
- Designation
- Cost center
- Profit center

## Functional Requirements

- Create and maintain the enterprise hierarchy
- Enforce approval and reporting relationships
- Support tenant-level organization scoping
- Provide reporting and dashboard views for structure health

## UI Flow

- Dashboard
- List pages for master data
- Detail pages for hierarchies
- Wizard-driven setup for new org units
- Organization chart view

## Platform Standards

- Multi-tenant scoping
- Audit trail for changes
- Role-based access control
- Workflow-based approvals

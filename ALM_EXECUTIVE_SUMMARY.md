## 🎯 Features Overview

### 1. Maturity Ladder Analysis
**Purpose:** Track when assets mature vs when liabilities come due

**Key Features:**
- 12 time bucket analysis (Day 1 to 5+ years)
- Automatic gap calculation
- Visual distribution charts
- Risk indicators by period
- Export to Excel

**Who Benefits:** Treasury managers, CFO

### 2. Gap Analysis (4 Types)
**Purpose:** Identify mismatches across multiple risk dimensions

**Gap Types:**
1. Liquidity Gap - Can we meet obligations?
2. Interest Rate Gap - How sensitive are we to rate changes?
3. Maturity Gap - Are maturities aligned?
4. Duration Gap - What's our interest rate risk?

**Who Benefits:** Risk managers, Treasury head

### 3. Liquidity Ratios (20+ Metrics)
**Purpose:** Monitor regulatory compliance and liquidity health

**Key Ratios:**
- **LCR** - Liquidity Coverage Ratio (Basel III)
- **NSFR** - Net Stable Funding Ratio (Basel III)
- **SLR** - Statutory Liquidity Ratio (RBI)
- **CRR** - Cash Reserve Ratio (RBI)
- Plus 16+ additional liquidity metrics

**Who Benefits:** Compliance officers, CFO, Board

### 4. Interest Rate Risk Analysis
**Purpose:** Assess impact of interest rate changes on earnings and equity

**Scenarios Tested:**
- Base case (current rates)
- Rate shocks: ±100 bps, ±200 bps
- Gradual changes: rise/fall over 12 months

**Impact Measured:**
- Net Interest Income (NII)
- Economic Value of Equity (EVE)
- Duration and repricing gaps

**Who Benefits:** ALCO members, CFO, Board

### 5. Quarterly Returns Management
**Purpose:** Streamline regulatory reporting with workflow

**Capabilities:**
- SLS (Supervisory Liquidity Statement) preparation
- IRS (Interest Rate Sensitivity) preparation
- 4-state approval workflow (Draft → Submitted → Approved/Rejected)
- Complete audit trail
- Export for regulatory submission

**Who Benefits:** Compliance team, CFO, Auditors

### 6. Alert Management
**Purpose:** Proactive monitoring with immediate notifications

**Alert Types:**
- Threshold breaches (LCR, NSFR, etc.)
- Gap limit violations
- Risk level escalations
- Regulatory compliance issues

**Severity Levels:**
- 🔴 Critical - Immediate action
- 🟠 High - 24 hour response
- 🟡 Medium - 2-3 day review
- 🔵 Low - Informational

**Who Benefits:** All ALM stakeholders

### 7. Executive Dashboard
**Purpose:** Single-pane view of ALM health

**Shows:**
- Key metrics at a glance
- Compliance status
- Active risk alerts
- Gap summaries
- Quick navigation

**Who Benefits:** CEO, CFO, Board members
